AI-Algorithms

AI-Algorithms is platform to help understand how K-Nearest Neighbors and K-Means Algorithms work.The objective of the project is to improve access to information related to the KNN and Kmeans algorithms. The page will present relevant information about the algorithms, as well as exercises related to the algorithm, the goal is for the user to have access to this information and to have feedback on what was learned during the page access.

Artificial Intelligence

In computer science, artificial intelligence (AI), sometimes called machine intelligence, is intelligence demonstrated by machines, in contrast to the natural intelligence displayed by humans and animals.

K-Nearest Neighbors

In pattern recognition, the k-nearest neighbors algorithm (k-NN) is a non-parametric method used for classification and regression. In both cases, the input consists of the k closest training examples in the feature space.

K-Means

k-means clustering is a method of vector quantization, originally from signal processing, that aims to partition n observations into k clusters in which each observation belongs to the cluster with the nearest mean (cluster centers or cluster centroid), serving as a prototype of the cluster.
